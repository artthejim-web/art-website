About Me

Jim Mbugua

I am an artist passionate about creating vibrant and expressive artwork. My journey began years ago when I was still young. I have always loved art given that my Dad is a visual artist. Over the years, through his help, I have honed my skills and I continue doing so. My work is inspired by the need to bring something unique to the market, as well as expressing myself through art. I also hope to inspire you all and help you see the beauty of life and art. Explore my gallery and vlogs to see my creative process!
